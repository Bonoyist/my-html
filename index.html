What is SPA?

The Special Program in the Arts (SPA) is a nationwide curriculum designed to nurture the talents of students in the fields of Music, Visual Arts, Media Arts, Dance, Theater, and Creative Writing. It provides young artists with specialized training while continuing academic excellence.
